Questions

How does Retriev connect to Stripe?

You connect via Stripe OAuth with one click. Retriev receives read-write access to payment events. Setup takes under 2 minutes and requires no code changes.

What's the success fee based on?

The success fee applies only to payments we directly recover — payments that failed and were subsequently captured through Retriev's retry or email flow. We track this precisely via Stripe webhooks.

Will recovery emails annoy customers?

Our AI calibrates to be helpful, not spammy. Emails are timed thoughtfully, personalized to each customer, and capped at sensible frequency limits. Customers respond well because the emails feel human.

How quickly will I see recovered revenue?

Most customers see their first recovery within 48 hours of connecting. Average recovery rates reach 60-75% within 30 days as the AI calibrates to your customer base.

Is Retriev GDPR compliant?

Yes. All emails include proper unsubscribe mechanisms. Customer data is processed under a DPA. We're hosted on Cloudflare infrastructure with EU data residency options available on Scale plan.

Can I customize the email templates?

On Growth and Scale plans, you can customize brand voice, colors, and tone. The AI still personalizes content per customer — you control the guardrails.
