FAQ
Questions answered
How does Retriev connect to Stripe?
You connect via Stripe OAuth — one click, read-write access to payment events. We never store your Stripe secret key. The entire setup takes under 5 minutes and requires no code changes.
What's the success fee based on?
The success fee applies only to payments we directly recover — i.e., a payment that failed and was subsequently captured through Retriev's retry or email flow. We track this precisely via Stripe webhooks.
Will recovery emails annoy my customers?
No — our AI is calibrated to be helpful, not spammy. Emails are timed thoughtfully, personalised to each customer, and capped at sensible frequency limits. Customers respond well because the emails feel human.
How quickly will I see recovered revenue?
Most customers see their first recovery within 48 hours of connecting. Average recovery rates reach 60–75% within 30 days as the AI calibrates to your customer base.
Is Retriev GDPR compliant?
Yes. All emails include proper unsubscribe mechanisms. Customer data is processed under a DPA. We're hosted on Cloudflare infrastructure with EU data residency options available on Scale plan.
Can I customise the email templates?
On Growth and Scale plans, you can customise brand voice, colours, and tone. The AI still personalises content per customer — you just control the guardrails and brand identity.
