How does Retriev connect to Stripe?

You connect via Stripe OAuth with one click. Retriev receives read-write access to payment events. Setup takes under 2 minutes and requires no code changes.

What does the success fee cover?

The success fee applies only to payments we directly recover — payments that failed and were subsequently captured through Retriev's retry or email flow.

How quickly will I see results?

Most customers see their first recovery within 48 hours of connecting. Average recovery rates reach 60-75% within 30 days as the AI calibrates.

Is Retriev GDPR compliant?

Yes. All emails include proper unsubscribe mechanisms. Customer data is processed under a DPA. We're hosted on Cloudflare infrastructure.
