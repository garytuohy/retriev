Pricing FAQ

Common questions about how pricing works
How does the success fee work? ↓
The success fee applies only to payments we directly recover — defined as a payment that failed and was subsequently captured within our recovery window. We track this precisely through Stripe webhooks and show you a full breakdown in your dashboard each month.
Is there a free trial? ↓
Yes — all plans include a 14-day free trial. No credit card required to start. You'll only be billed after the trial period ends, and we'll remind you 3 days before.
What happens if I exceed my MRR limit? ↓
We'll notify you when you're approaching your MRR cap and help you upgrade. We won't suddenly cut off your recovery sequences — you'll have a 7-day grace period to upgrade before any impact.
Can I cancel anytime? ↓
Absolutely. Cancel anytime with no questions asked. You'll retain access until the end of your billing period. We don't do lock-in contracts on monthly plans. Annual plans are non-refundable after 30 days.
Do you offer discounts for startups? ↓
Yes — we offer 50% off for the first 6 months for YC, Techstars, and other recognised accelerator alumni. Email us with proof of acceptance and we'll apply the discount manually.
