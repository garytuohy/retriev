Every failed payment is a customer at risk

The average SaaS company loses 4-6% of revenue to failed payments every month. That's millions of dollars left on the table — and customers quietly churning because a card expired.

Most founders don't even realize it's happening. They see churn in their metrics but don't know why. The truth? A huge portion of "churned" customers never wanted to leave. They just had a payment issue.

Retriev exists to fix this. We automatically detect failed payments, reach out to customers with personalized emails at optimal times, and recover the revenue before the customer even notices there was a problem.

Our Story

Retriev started with a simple observation: one of our founders was running a SaaS business and noticed something strange in their Stripe dashboard. They were losing 5% of revenue every month to failed payments, but their "churn" metrics only showed 2%.

Where was the other 3% going? It wasn't customers choosing to leave. It was cards expiring, banks declining, temporary holds — things that could be fixed if someone just reached out.

They built a simple script to send follow-up emails when payments failed. Recovery jumped from 45% to 78% in two weeks. That script became Retriev.

Today, we've helped hundreds of SaaS companies recover millions in lost revenue. Our AI-powered system doesn't just send emails — it learns when each customer is most likely to respond, what tone works best, and how to turn a payment failure into a positive customer experience.
